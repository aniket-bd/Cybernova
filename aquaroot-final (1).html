How It Works

From soil to sprinkler
in milliseconds

1. Sense
Wireless probes buried at root depth measure moisture, temperature, conductivity, and pH — continuously.
2. Analyse
AquaRoot AI cross-references live readings with crop profiles, growth stage, and local weather forecasts.
3. Decide
A precision schedule is generated — zone by zone, drop by drop — delivering exactly what each crop needs.
4. Grow
Smart valves open on command. Results loop back into the model — every cycle makes the next one smarter.
