Core Features

Everything your fields need,
nothing they don't

🌡️
Real-Time Soil Sensing
Sub-surface sensors report moisture, temperature, and nutrient levels every 60 seconds, triggering irrigation only when truly needed.
🤖
AI Scheduling Engine
Our model fuses 14-day weather forecasts, evapotranspiration rates, and crop growth stages to predict optimal watering windows.
⚡
Instant Valve Control
Control every valve on your farm remotely from a browser or phone. Automation rules, manual override, and emergency shutoff — all in one tap.
📊
Usage Analytics
Detailed daily, weekly, and seasonal water consumption breakdowns. Compare zones, benchmark against regional averages, export reports.
🌧️
Rain Prediction Skip
Automatically suspends irrigation cycles when rain is forecast, resuming only once actual soil readings confirm adequate moisture.
🔔
Smart Alerts
Get notified about dry spells, leaks, clogged emitters, sensor faults, and abnormal pressure — before they become costly problems.
How It Works

From sensor to sprinkler
in milliseconds

1
Sense
Wireless soil probes continuously measure moisture, temperature, and conductivity at root depth.
2
Analyse
The AquaRoot AI cross-references live readings with crop needs, weather data, and historical patterns.
3
Decide
A precision schedule is generated — zone by zone — delivering exactly the right amount of water.
4
Act
Smart valves open on command. Results feed back into the model, making every cycle smarter.
